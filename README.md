## 🏆 Certification Facts

- Administered by ISC2 — the world's largest non-profit cybersecurity membership organisation
- Framework spans all disciplines of information security (the Common Body of Knowledge)
- CAT format: the exam adapts to your performance — harder questions mean you're doing well
- Passing score: **700/1000** scaled score — not a raw percentage
- Experience requirement: **5 years** in 2+ of the 8 domains (university degree waives 1 year)
- After passing: requires **endorsement** by an ISC2 member within 9 months
- Maintenance: **120 CPEs** required every 3-year cycle to stay certified
- Recommended prerequisite: CompTIA Security+ or equivalent hands-on security experience
